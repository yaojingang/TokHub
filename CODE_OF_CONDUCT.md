# Code of Conduct

TokHub contributors are expected to keep collaboration respectful, technical,
and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Keep issue and pull request discussion on the specific code, design, test, or
  documentation change.
- Respect privacy. Do not post credentials, production logs, customer data, or
  private screenshots.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, tokens, or non-public operational details.
- Repeated off-topic promotion or spam.
- Deliberately disruptive pull requests or issues.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues, block users, or
reject contributions that violate these rules.

Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`, not public issues.
